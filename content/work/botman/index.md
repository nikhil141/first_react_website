Simple, great looking and easy to use audio book player to play books already present on your phone.

#### Features:

- Dark theme.
- Playback speed control.
- Simple and intuitive design.
- Seek time control.

#### Formats supported:

- mp3
- m4a
- aac
- ogg
- m4b
